A Phishing Awareness Platform

Learn the signs. Trust your clicks.

Phishing is one of the most common and dangerous cyber threats — and it often works by tricking people, not systems. From fake login pages to urgent emails, these scams are getting harder to spot.

Hooked? is designed to raise awareness through interactive learning, real-world resources, and gamified challenges. By understanding the signs of phishing, users become more confident and better prepared to protect themselves and their organisation.
